Kian — A Soul That Refuses to Shrink Just to Belong

Part I – The Fire That Sees The desert didn’t have sand. It had glass. Shards of ancient light, melted by time, cracked under Kian’s boots. He walked for what felt like days — alone — the final piece heavy in his pocket, humming so faintly he wasn’t sure if it was real or his own bones unraveling. Then he saw it.

People told him to soften his edges. To laugh a little quieter. To nod more. But inside him burned something too ancient to silence...
